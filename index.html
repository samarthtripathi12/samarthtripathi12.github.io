Modeling the universe — from black holes to neural networks.

Newton had less. Einstein had more. Both changed everything.

But physics has been stagnating. The Standard Model is 50 years old. Dark energy is a placeholder — we named something we cannot explain and moved on. The WOW signal was detected in 1977, lasted 72 seconds, and has never been explained. We have been waiting 47 years for a repeat that has not come.

Reality is not just beyond our reach. It is beyond our comprehension by design. The universe does not owe us clean equations.

This is where I work.

Uncertainty is not the enemy of science. It is the force that drives it — the same force bending light around a black hole, burying a gravitational wave in noise, scattering four Markov chains across weight space before pulling them all to the same truth. Beneath every experiment is the same question: is there geometry hiding inside the chaos?

I compute because I believe the answer is yes. And I believe the next person to find it will not be waiting for institutional permission to start looking.
