Spacetime Warping — 5 Simultaneous Panels

Schwarzschild Metric · RK4 · Geodesic Integration · From First Principles

Watch the fabric of spacetime itself curve as mass "switches on" — the grid warping is a direct visualization of the Schwarzschild metric ds² = -(1-Rₛ/r)c²dt² + dr²/(1-Rₛ/r) + r²dΩ². Photons follow the curved geodesics in real time. GR bending vs Newtonian side by side reveals why Einstein's 1915 prediction doubled Newton's. Effective potential V(r) shows why the photon sphere exists at r=1.5Rₛ as a local maximum.

2→6→4→1 Architecture · Backpropagation · Probabilistic · Markov-like
